About the meeting

Leading the second half of IA2030 with stronger country ownership

The 2026 East and Southern Africa EPI Managers Meeting brings together national immunization programme leaders, partners and technical experts to review progress, exchange practical experience and agree on priority actions for resilient immunization systems.

The meeting will place country leadership, implementation realities and peer learning at the centre of discussions.
